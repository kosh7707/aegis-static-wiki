# Wiki Taxonomy

## Top-level buckets
- `wiki/canon/charter/` — platform charter and global rules
- `wiki/canon/specs/` — migrated service and system specifications
- `wiki/canon/api/` — migrated API contracts
- `wiki/canon/handoff/` — future migrated handoff material
- `wiki/canon/roadmap/` — future migrated roadmap material
- `wiki/canon/work-requests/` — future migrated negotiation/request artifacts
- `wiki/canon/feedback/` — future migrated external feedback
- `wiki/context/decisions/` — decision rationale and canonicality explanations
- `wiki/context/services/` — service-oriented synthesized pages
- `wiki/context/detection/` — future S4 detection-engineering knowledge pages
- `wiki/context/timelines/` — future chronology pages
- `wiki/context/regressions/` — future regression knowledge pages
- `wiki/context/open-questions/` — future unresolved questions

## Page-family rule
- Canonical pages preserve source meaning and provenance.
- Context pages help agents answer “why”, “how does this connect”, and “what changed”.

## Migration rule
Migration is bucket-by-bucket. During transition, [[wiki/system/migration-map]] is the authoritative path-resolution ledger.
